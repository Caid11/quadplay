quadplay✜ supports keyboard, touch controls, and Xbox One, Xbox 360, PS4, 8BitDo, Switch, and Stadia controllers. In game, press the **"P"** key or **"☰"**, **"Options"**, or **"Start"** controller button to pause and switch controllers.
